Fixed Joint countertrack

GKN Automotive's CountertrackTMfixed joint uses the principle of “opposed tracks” for significant efficiency improvement and packaging reduction. It combines this with a specific track profile in longitudinal direction to allow angles of up to 52 degrees. This breakthrough technology sets the new benchmark for constant velocity joints (CVJ).

Benefits

  • High Efficiency
  • Optimum Packaging
  • Compared to conventional Rzeppatype joints
  • Reduction of losses by 50 -60%
  • Weight down of up to 15%
  • Packaging reduction of up to 8%

Technical features

  • 50°(52°Max) Articulation angle
  • 8 Ball Joint
  • Countertrack Technology with S-shape track design

Operating Principle

Designed for FWD and AWD applications with efficiency improvement of the entire drivetrain is required.
